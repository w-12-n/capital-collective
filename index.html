About

Entrepreneurs complete a single application on Capital Collective, sharing their details and vision. We instantly share the application with a network of venture funds, giving founders the chance to reach multiple investors with a single click. Each fund reviews the application and connects directly with founders they’re interested in.

For venture funds, Capital Collective offers a way to discover promising founders at the earliest stages of their journey. Funds also gain visibility by joining a larger network, helping them connect with founders they might otherwise miss.
